Mobile application for farriers and hoof trimmers

We are happy to introduce you our awesome application designed and tested by professional farriers. We were using it for over 5 years now and have improved it a lot.

Starting with just a simple "notebook" resemble app, it grew up in the mature tool that allows you to keep your records in a very well organized way and access your data easily.

Here are some major features:
  • Create clients, locations (aka stables), horses and jobs(your actual trims/shoeings) in just a couple clicks
  • Standalone app. No internet connection required to operate
  • Search horses by name, owner name
  • Show horses filtered by owner or location
  • Show events awaiting for payments
  • Show your income breakdown by years
  • Show your general statistics
  • Take, store, export to and import from your phone's library and show photos of horses' hooves separately for every single job and/or for the horse
  • Export your database to and import it from Google Drive(Coming soon).

Our TODO list is full of new fancy features like reminders and issues selector that we are going to implement soon.

If you have found a bug or faced any issues with the app please send us an email and we will give you a hand or fix it ASAP.
